CLAN DESCRIPTION:

 

ACTIVE WAR CLAN.  Adult clan est. 8/14.  Donate before you request.  Ratio 1:2. Mandatory to use both attacks in war. MUST donate mininum of 200 troops per season.  Elder and Co-leader is earned.  Leaders decide who goes to war.

CLAN RULES:

 

  • Must have level 3 and up troops

  • TH 6 & up with gold walls

  • MUST have war expierence

  • MUST Donate mininum of 200 troops per season

  • MUST BE ACTIVE

  • Be respectful to all members

  • Only donate what is requested

  • level 3 and higher archers default

  • <<NEW MEMBERS>> may be asked to show two different attacks in replay before being allowed to war.

WAR RULES:

 

  1. MUST BE ACTIVE

  2. MUST USE BOTH ATTACKS IN WAR     (if a player does not use both attacks in war then they will be opted out next war)

  3. NO TOWN HALL SNIPE'n

  4. LEADERS FILL WAR CLAN CASTLE

  5. Strategies change each war so speak to a leader before attacking

  6. OPTING out is an option for war but leaders have sole discretion of who will war based on clan needs and balance.

  7. If any player (excluding multiple accounts) needs to opt out of war for more than one week then that player will need to notify a leader beforehand.
